Gaps & Conflicts

Auto-detected ambiguity, missing Accountables, and low-confidence responses are surfaced here for action.

Resolve issues in the wizard or assign temporary owners with due dates. Nothing disappears without a decision.

Reports

Executive-friendly views that summarize completion, risk, and accountability coverage.

Every report explains what changed, what is unresolved, and what leadership needs to sign off.

Exports

One-click outputs: Executive Summary (HTML/PDF), RACI Matrix (Excel), Gap Register, Decision Log.

Exports preserve the canonical Excel structure and append workshop metadata for traceability.

Settings

Configure storage, permissions, and facilitation preferences. Everything is self-hosted and lives in your browser or local network.

Storage

Use localStorage by default. Toggle IndexedDB for heavier scenarios. No external SaaS dependencies.

Facilitation defaults

  • Require executive sign-off on every workshop.
  • Flag multiple Accountables as conflicts.
  • Force confidence slider before saving.

Everything here is optional. The app runs fully offline with no additional setup.
